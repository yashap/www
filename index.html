Noble Inc. is proud to offer education credits towards the IIBA’s™ CBAP™ certification and Continuing Education Units (CDUs) towards re-certification.

 

 

What can Noble Inc. do for you?

We offer courses and complete training programs in Business Analysis and Business Analysis consulting services. Our courses are IIBA-approved for BABOK2 and qualify for credits (CDUs) toward CBAP certification. For in-house training (at your site), anywhere on the globe, please contact us directly via email at info@nobleinc.ca. For information about public courses in the U.S. and Canada, please visit our network of Noble Inc. affiliates.

Noble Inc. also provides a turn-key solution for corporate education centres, colleges and universities that wish to add certified Business Analysis training to their curriculum, through our affiliates program. We provide our affiliates with course materials (both basic and advanced BA programs), experienced instructors, marketing support as well as a train-the-trainer program (for centres that wish to use their own instructors). Please contact Noble Inc. to become an affiliate.
